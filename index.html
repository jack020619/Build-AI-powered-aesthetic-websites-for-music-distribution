Frequently asked questions

How much does Six Eyes cost? +

Six Eyes is free to start — the Starter plan is $0 forever and keeps 80% of royalties. Pro Artist is $1.99/month and Label is $4.99/month; both let you keep 100% of your royalties with no commission.

How many platforms do you distribute to? +

We deliver your music to 150+ stores and streaming services, including Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, Deezer and Tidal.

How fast does my music go live? +

Releases typically go live on the major platforms within 24–48 hours on paid plans. You can also schedule a future release date and run pre-save campaigns ahead of it.

Do I keep ownership of my music? +

Always. You keep 100% ownership of your masters and publishing rights. Six Eyes never claims any ownership of your music.

What's the minimum to withdraw my earnings? +

You can withdraw starting at just $5 — far below the $25+ minimum many competitors require — so you get paid sooner.
